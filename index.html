Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

ExpenseManager is a simple and secure personal finance tracking application that helps you manage your income and expenses. Your privacy is important to us.

All financial data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Transaction Records

  • Amount
  • Transaction type (Income/Expense)
  • Category
  • Notes
  • Date and time stamps

User Preferences

  • Reminder settings
  • Notification preferences

2. How We Store Your Data

  • All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room Database.
  • No cloud backup — your data never leaves your device.
  • No user accounts — we do not collect usernames, emails, passwords, or personal identifiers.
  • No analytics — we do not track usage patterns or user behavior.

3. Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you manually delete transactions or uninstall the app.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

4. Permissions

Permissions Requested

1. POST_NOTIFICATIONS

  • Purpose: To send reminder notifications for adding expenses or income.
  • Optional: You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings.

2. RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

  • Purpose: To reschedule reminder notifications after device restart.
  • Benefit: Ensures reminders continue working after reboot.

Permissions NOT Requested

  • ❌ No Internet access — your data stays offline.
  • ❌ No Location access.
  • ❌ No Camera access.
  • ❌ No Contacts access.
  • ❌ No external storage access (except app-specific storage).

5. Children's Privacy

ExpenseManager does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

  • No data is transmitted from the device.
  • No user accounts are required.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • All data remains stored locally on the user's device.

While the app is safe for users of all ages, we recommend parental guidance for children under 13 to help them understand financial management concepts.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
